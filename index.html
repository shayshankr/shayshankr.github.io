Hello, I'm Shayshank.

A BI & Data Engineer who shipped his way into AI and mobile. Multi-disciplinary by nature, comfortable across data, AI, web, and Android.

I grew up across India — primary school in Chennai, secondary in Pune, college and early career in Delhi, with time in Udaipur — and most recently lived a year in Dublin, Ireland completing my MSc in AI with First Class Honours (1:1).

For five years at Datafortune Software Solutions, I designed Azure Data Factory pipelines, built Snowflake / SQL Server warehouses, and shipped Power BI dashboards for international clients (US healthcare, UK NLP company, Canadian car reinsurance). I was the bridge between developers and clients — translating asks into BRDs, running standups across IST and EST, and holding 100% SLA compliance.

Then I built things in public. An AI visa-decision tracker that grew to 4,300+ users with zero marketing budget. A psychrometric chart calculator for HVAC engineers. An MSc thesis on Hybrid Reinforcement Learning for diabetes care. And in the last month alone, 20+ new GitHub repos shipping a 5-embassy visa-checker product suite to the web, Android, and iOS.

I write to learn — 3,820 LinkedIn followers, published articles on data, AI, and ops drawn from real life. I think the best engineers are also great communicators, and I try to be both.
